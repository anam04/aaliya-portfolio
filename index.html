About Me

My research focuses on how early life adversity impacts stress pathways and cellular aging. With training in both wet lab and computational techniques, she investigates telomere biology, gene regulation, and neuroendocrine mechanisms. I have previously conducted research at NTU Singapore, UAB, and Shiv Nadar University. I am passionate about bridging molecular biology with public health to inform science-driven solutions.
